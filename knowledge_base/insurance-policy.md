# Wayfare Trip Protection (Travel Insurance)

## What It Is

Wayfare Trip Protection is an optional add-on, purchasable at checkout or up to 14 days after your initial booking, priced at roughly 6–8% of your total package cost. It bundles trip cancellation/interruption coverage, emergency medical coverage, baggage protection, and travel delay coverage into a single plan underwritten by Wayfare's insurance partner.

## Trip Cancellation & Interruption Coverage

Reimburses 100% of non-refundable trip costs if you must cancel or cut short your trip for a **covered reason**, including:

- Sudden illness or injury to you, a traveling companion, or an immediate family member, certified by a physician as preventing travel.
- Death of you, a traveling companion, or an immediate family member.
- Your home or destination becoming uninhabitable due to fire, flood, or other natural disaster.
- Involuntary job loss (layoff), if you've been continuously employed for at least 1 year.
- A government-issued travel advisory or mandatory evacuation order covering your destination, issued after you purchased the plan.
- Jury duty or a legal subpoena you cannot postpone.

Coverage is **not** provided for cancellations due to a pre-existing medical condition unless the plan was purchased within 14 days of the initial trip deposit (a "pre-existing condition waiver" applies in that case).

## Emergency Medical & Evacuation

Up to $50,000 in emergency medical expense coverage and up to $250,000 in emergency medical evacuation coverage while traveling more than 100 miles from home. This is supplemental to, not a replacement for, your regular health insurance — claims should be filed with your primary insurer first where applicable.

## Baggage Protection

Up to $2,500 per traveler for lost, stolen, or damaged baggage and personal effects (subject to a $100 per-item sub-limit for electronics and a $500 per-item sub-limit for jewelry). Also covers up to $500 in reimbursement for essential items (toiletries, a change of clothes) purchased during a baggage delay of 12 or more hours, as documented by receipts.

## Travel Delay Coverage

If your trip is delayed 6 or more hours by a covered reason (weather, mechanical failure, carrier-caused delay), Wayfare Trip Protection reimburses up to $200 per day (max $1,000) for reasonable additional expenses: meals, lodging, and local transportation.

## What's Not Covered

- Cancellations for reasons not listed above (e.g., simple change of plans, being denied a visa due to late application, financial hardship not tied to job loss).
- Extreme sports or activities not disclosed at the time of purchase.
- Traveling against an active government advisory that was already in effect when you booked.
- Losses due to your own negligence (e.g., leaving bags unattended in a public place).

## How to File a Claim

Claims must be filed within 30 days of the covered event via the Wayfare claims portal, with supporting documentation (medical notes, police reports for theft, receipts for delay-related purchases, or the relevant advisory/order). Most claims are processed within 10–15 business days of receiving complete documentation.
