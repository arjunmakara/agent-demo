# Wayfare Cancellation & Refund Policy

## Standard Cancellation Windows

Bookings made through Wayfare fall into three refund tiers based on how far in advance you cancel, relative to the trip's departure date:

- **72+ hours before departure**: Full refund to the original payment method, minus a $25 per-traveler processing fee. Refunds are issued within 5–7 business days.
- **24–72 hours before departure**: 50% refund of the total trip cost. No processing fee is deducted.
- **Less than 24 hours before departure, or no-show**: No refund. The booking is forfeited in full.

These windows apply to the *package* booking made through Wayfare (flights + lodging + activities bundled). Airlines and hotels booked independently follow their own cancellation terms, which Wayfare will surface separately at checkout.

## Flexible / Refundable Fares

Trips booked under a "Flexible" fare (identified at checkout, typically 15–20% more expensive than Standard fares) can be cancelled up to **12 hours before departure** for a full refund with no processing fee. Flexible fares also allow one free date change per booking.

## Cancelling Due to Illness, Family Emergency, or Weather

If you cancel due to documented illness (yours or an immediate family member's), a family emergency, or a government-issued travel advisory covering your destination, Wayfare waives the standard refund tiers above and issues a full refund or a travel credit (your choice), regardless of how close to departure you cancel. Supporting documentation (doctor's note, death certificate, or official advisory link) must be submitted within 14 days of the cancellation request.

## Trip Insurance and Cancellation

Travelers who purchased Wayfare Trip Protection (see the insurance policy) are not subject to the standard cancellation tiers above for covered reasons — see the insurance policy document for the list of covered cancellation reasons and claim process.

## How to Cancel

Cancellations must be submitted through the Wayfare account dashboard or by contacting support at least 1 hour before the applicable deadline above. Verbal cancellation requests (phone/chat) are confirmed by email and the timestamp of that confirmation email is used to determine which refund tier applies.

## Partial Cancellations (Multi-Traveler Bookings)

For bookings with multiple travelers, individual travelers may be removed from a booking under the same tiered refund rules above, applied per-traveler. Removing a traveler may change the per-person price for remaining travelers (e.g., losing a group discount) — Wayfare will disclose any price adjustment before confirming a partial cancellation.
